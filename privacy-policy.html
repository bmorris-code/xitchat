XitChat Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Our Privacy Promise

XitChat is designed with privacy as our top priority. We believe in private communication without surveillance. Unlike other messaging apps, we don't collect your personal information, track your behavior, or store your data on our servers.

Data We Don't Collect

We DO NOT collect:

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data or GPS coordinates
  • ❌ Contact lists or address books
  • ❌ Message content or conversation history
  • ❌ Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • ❌ Device identifiers for tracking
  • ❌ Any data stored on external servers

How XitChat Works

XitChat uses decentralized mesh networking:

  • 🔗 Messages travel directly between devices
  • 📱 No central servers or data centers
  • 🔐 End-to-end encryption by default
  • 💾 Data stored only on your device
  • 🌐 Works without internet connection

Minimal Technical Data

To make mesh networking work, XitChat temporarily uses:

  • Bluetooth ID: For device discovery (not stored)
  • Local WiFi Info: For direct connections (not transmitted)
  • Local Storage: Messages stored only on your device
Important: This technical data never leaves your device and is deleted when you close the app.

Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • ✅ Right to Delete: Clear all app data anytime
  • ✅ Right to Export: Save your message history
  • ✅ Right to Opt-Out: No data collection to opt-out from
  • ✅ Right to Access: All data stored locally on your device

Security Measures

We protect your privacy with:

  • 🔒 AES-256 encryption for all messages
  • 🛡️ No server storage of user data
  • 🔐 Local device encryption
  • 📖 Open source code for transparency
  • 🚫 No third-party analytics or tracking

Children's Privacy

XitChat does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13. Since we don't collect data, COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements do not apply to our service.

International Users

Your privacy rights are protected regardless of your location:

  • GDPR (Europe): Full compliance - no data collection to regulate
  • CCPA (California): Complete privacy by design
  • Global: Same privacy standards for all users worldwide

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how XitChat handles data (which is highly unlikely given our privacy-first design), we will:

  • 📢 Update this privacy policy immediately
  • 🔔 Notify users in the app
  • 📧 Send email notifications to registered users (if applicable)
