## Full Description (4000 characters max)

### 🌐 Welcome to XitChat - The Future of Decentralized Communication

XitChat is a revolutionary mesh messaging app that works WITHOUT internet! Connect with people nearby using Bluetooth, WiFi Direct, and advanced peer-to-peer networking. Your messages, your network, your privacy.

### ✨ KEY FEATURES

**🔌 True Offline Messaging**
- Send messages without internet or cellular data
- Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) mesh networking
- WiFi Direct for high-speed local connections
- Messages hop through nearby devices to reach their destination

**🌍 Multiple Network Layers**
- Bluetooth Mesh: Ultra-low power, short-range (10-100m)
- WiFi Direct: High-speed, medium-range (up to 200m)
- Nostr Protocol: Global decentralized messaging
- Hybrid routing: Automatically picks the best connection

**🔐 Privacy & Security**
- End-to-end encryption for all messages
- No central servers - fully peer-to-peer
- Your data stays on your device
- Optional Tor routing for enhanced privacy
- Proof-of-Work spam protection

**💬 Rich Communication**
- Private 1-on-1 chats
- Public rooms and channels
- Location-based "Buzz" feed
- Real-time peer radar
- Voice notes and media sharing
- Emoji reactions and status updates

**🎮 Built-in Entertainment**
- Classic games (Snake, Tetris, Pong)
- Play offline with friends
- Leaderboards and achievements

**💰 XC Economy**
- Earn XC coins for participation
- Trade in the mesh marketplace
- Tip content creators
- Zero transaction fees

**📍 Geohash Channels**
- Auto-join local area channels
- Discover people nearby
- Location-based communities
- Privacy-preserving location sharing

**🎨 Retro-Futuristic Design**
- Inspired by classic South African chat apps (Mxit)
- CRT terminal aesthetic
- Multiple color themes
- Customizable avatars and moods

## Data Safety Questionnaire

**Does your app collect or share user data?**
- No, XitChat does not collect or share any user data

**Data Types**:
- Personal Info: None collected
- Financial Info: None collected
- Location: Used locally only, never transmitted to servers
- Messages: Encrypted, peer-to-peer only, not stored on servers
- Device ID: Generated locally, not linked to identity

**Security Practices**:
- Data encrypted in transit: Yes (end-to-end encryption)
- Data encrypted at rest: Yes (device storage encryption)
- Users can request data deletion: Yes (local data only)
- Independent security review: Planned

**Data Retention**:
- Messages: Stored locally on device only
- User can delete all data anytime from settings
