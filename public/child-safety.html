XitChat – Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 2026

XitChat is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

1. Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on XitChat:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Grooming, exploitation, or solicitation of minors
  • Any content that sexualises individuals under 18
  • Attempts to establish inappropriate contact with minors

2. Age Restriction

XitChat is not directed at children under 13 years of age. Users must be at least 13 years old to use the app. We do not knowingly allow minors under 13 to create accounts or use the service.

3. Reporting Mechanism

Users can report child safety concerns directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly. Any confirmed CSAM is reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) via the CyberTipline, as required by law, and to relevant regional and national authorities.

4. Enforcement

Accounts found to be involved in CSAE activity are immediately and permanently banned. We co-operate fully with law enforcement investigations.

5. Contact

To report a child safety concern or contact our designated safety representative:

Email: safety@xitchat.app

Our designated contact is prepared to speak about XitChat's CSAM prevention practices and compliance with applicable laws.
