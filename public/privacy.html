XitChat Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Overview

XitChat is a decentralized, serverless mesh messaging application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not operate servers that store your messages.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use advertising networks or analytics trackers.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your chosen display name and handle
  • Your cryptographic key pair (used for message signing on the Nostr network)
  • Your message history (stored in your browser's local storage)
  • App preferences and settings

4. Peer-to-Peer Communications

XitChat uses the following decentralized protocols to deliver messages:

  • Nostr protocol — Messages are relayed through public Nostr relay servers. Direct messages are end-to-end encrypted (NIP-04). Room messages are broadcast to relay servers.
  • WebRTC — Direct peer-to-peer connections for real-time messaging. Signaling is handled via Ably; no message content is stored by Ably.
  • Bluetooth BLE — Local device discovery and messaging. All communication stays within Bluetooth range and is not transmitted over the internet.
  • Wi-Fi Direct — Local network peer-to-peer messaging. All communication stays on the local network.

5. Permissions Used

  • Bluetooth — Required to discover and communicate with nearby XitChat users.
  • Location (foreground) — Required by Android for Bluetooth LE scanning. We do not store or transmit your location.
  • Camera — Used only for scanning QR invite codes. Photos are not stored or transmitted.
  • Nearby Wi-Fi Devices — Required for Wi-Fi Direct peer discovery on Android 12+.
  • Notifications — Used to alert you of incoming messages.

6. AI Assistant (XitBot)

XitChat includes an optional AI assistant powered by Groq and Google Gemini APIs. When you use XitBot, your message is sent to these third-party AI services to generate a response. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

XitBot usage is optional. You can use XitChat without ever interacting with the AI assistant.

7. Children's Privacy

XitChat is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the app.
